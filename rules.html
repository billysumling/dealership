Basketball Rules

  • Rule 1: No traveling
  • Rule 2: No double dribble
  • Rule 3: No goaltending
  • Rule 4: No carrying

How to Play Basketball

To play basketball, follow these steps:

  1. Gather a team of players.
  2. Set up a basketball court with hoops at each end.
  3. Choose a basketball and assign positions to players.
  4. Start the game by tossing the basketball into the air at center court.
  5. Players dribble the ball by bouncing it off the floor while moving towards the opposing team's hoop.
  6. Shoot the ball into the hoop to score points.
  7. Defend your own hoop by blocking shots and stealing the ball from the opposing team.
  8. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
